    Expert oncology care accepted by major Israeli health insurance providers

    At Dr. Esti Tchoubar's gastrointestinal oncology clinic, we provide specialized treatment for all types of digestive system cancers. Our comprehensive services are covered by major health insurance plans including Maccabi, Leumit, and Meuhedet through Form 17 referrals. Each treatment plan is personalized to the patient's specific diagnosis, stage, and individual needs, ensuring the best possible outcomes through evidence-based medicine and compassionate care.

    Our united healthcare providers approach means seamless coordination between diagnostic services, treatment planning, and ongoing support. We work closely with your referring physician and insurance provider to ensure continuity of care throughout your treatment journey.

    Esophageal Cancer Treatment

    Esophageal cancer requires prompt diagnosis and specialized treatment planning. Our clinic provides comprehensive care for both squamous cell carcinoma and adenocarcinoma of the esophagus. We utilize advanced diagnostic techniques including endoscopic ultrasound and PET-CT imaging to accurately stage disease and develop personalized treatment strategies.

    Treatment options may include chemotherapy, radiation therapy, targeted therapy, or immunotherapy depending on the cancer stage and location. Our coordinating nurse ensures patients understand their treatment plan and manages any side effects that may arise during therapy. We work closely with thoracic surgeons when surgical intervention is appropriate, ensuring multidisciplinary care coordination.

    Home health care agencies can be coordinated through our clinic to provide additional support during treatment, ensuring patients receive comprehensive care in the comfort of their homes when appropriate.

    Gastric Cancer Management

    Gastric cancer treatment requires a nuanced approach that considers tumor location, histology, and molecular characteristics. Our clinic specializes in both early-stage and advanced gastric malignancies, offering patients access to the latest treatment protocols and clinical trials when appropriate.

    We provide comprehensive staging workups including endoscopic evaluation, imaging studies, and molecular profiling to guide treatment decisions. Therapy may involve perioperative chemotherapy, targeted agents like HER2 inhibitors for eligible patients, or immunotherapy for microsatellite instability-high tumors.

    Our healthcare team coordinates with surgical oncologists for patients who are candidates for gastrectomy, ensuring optimal timing of surgery within the treatment sequence. Nutritional support is integrated into care plans, recognizing the unique challenges gastric cancer patients face.

    Small Intestine Cancer Care

    Small intestine cancers, while rare, require specialized expertise in diagnosis and management. Our clinic treats adenocarcinomas, neuroendocrine tumors, lymphomas, and sarcomas of the small bowel with individualized treatment approaches based on tumor type and extent of disease.

    Diagnostic evaluation includes capsule endoscopy, enteroscopy, and advanced imaging to accurately localize and characterize tumors. Treatment strategies are tailored to the specific histology, with options including surgery, chemotherapy, targeted therapy, and radiation when indicated.

    For neuroendocrine tumors, we provide access to somatostatin analogs and peptide receptor radionuclide therapy (PRRT) when appropriate. Our multidisciplinary approach ensures patients benefit from the collective expertise of medical oncology, surgical oncology, and interventional radiology.

    Colorectal Cancer Treatment

    Colorectal cancer is one of the most common gastrointestinal malignancies, and our clinic provides comprehensive care across all stages of disease. We specialize in both colon and rectal cancers, recognizing the distinct treatment approaches required for each location.

    Our treatment protocols incorporate molecular testing to identify actionable mutations and guide therapy selection. For metastatic disease, we offer sequential treatment strategies using chemotherapy combinations, targeted agents (anti-EGFR and anti-VEGF antibodies), and immunotherapy for microsatellite instability-high or mismatch repair deficient tumors.

    Coordination with colorectal surgeons ensures optimal timing of surgical intervention, whether for primary tumor resection or metastasectomy. For rectal cancer, we work closely with radiation oncologists to deliver neoadjuvant chemoradiation when indicated, maximizing sphincter preservation opportunities.

    Pancreatic Cancer Expertise

    Pancreatic cancer presents unique challenges requiring specialized expertise and rapid treatment initiation. Our clinic provides comprehensive care for both resectable and advanced pancreatic adenocarcinoma, as well as neuroendocrine tumors of the pancreas.

    We utilize advanced imaging including pancreatic protocol CT and MRI to accurately stage disease and determine resectability. For borderline resectable tumors, we offer neoadjuvant therapy protocols to optimize surgical outcomes. In advanced disease, we provide access to modern chemotherapy regimens including FOLFIRINOX and gemcitabine-based combinations.

    Our coordinating nurse plays a crucial role in managing the complex side effects of pancreatic cancer treatment, including pancreatic enzyme supplementation, pain management, and nutritional support. We coordinate closely with hepatobiliary surgeons for patients who are surgical candidates.
